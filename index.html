One Winter break my daughter hoorain drew a picture of a rainbow dress. When I saw it I excitedly said, “I can make that!” Nearly $100 of fabric and 12 hours of work later, hoorain put on the finished dress and exclaimed, “I’m wearing my imagination!”

After seeing her excitement we wanted other children to experience the gift of wearing their imagination but knew the cost and amount of work weren’t practical for everyone.
